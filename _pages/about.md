* I received my Ph.D. in Faculty of Architecture, Civil And Transportation Engineering at Beijing University of Technology, supervised by [Prof. Wei Wang](https://facte.bjut.edu.cn/info/1087/1673.htm) and [Prof. Xiufeng Pang](https://baike.baidu.com/item/%E9%80%84%E7%A7%80%E9%94%8B/22228377?fr=aladdin), in 2021. I received my B.Eng. degree in College of Architecture and Civil Engineering at Beijing University of Technology in 2015.
* I am proficient in EnergyPlus, jEPlus, GenOpt and Modelica, and related building simulations, and have some experience in programming with Python.
* My current research interest is on building simulation and building control. My broad research interests focus on building retrofitting and commissioning.
